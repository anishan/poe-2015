A look back on Lazer Maze

Posted by the Spice Girls on December 17, 2015

We began this project way back in October, and time has certainly flown by. Just for kicks, here's a blow-by-blow of the making of Lazer Maze.

Sprint 1

Tasks included:

  • Deciding on a project
  • Outlining personal learning goals
  • Learning about laser safety
  • Establishing the importance of modularity in a large system
  • Learning to delegate tasks to sub-teams
  • Launching our website

Here are some of our initial sketches from Sprint 1.



Sprint 2

Tasks included:

  • Building two tabletop prototypes
  • Drafting the bill of materials
  • Designing the box structure
  • Implementing a small-scale version of the electrical system
  • Designing the user interface

Sprint 3

Tasks included:

  • Purchasing materials
  • Building the box
  • Developing the software and user interface
  • Wiring the electrical system
  • Testing sensing
  • Designing and fabricating laser mounts


... and here is where we are now.

In this past sprint, our tasks included:

  • Finishing the software and user interface
  • Integrating the electrical and software systems
  • Designing and fabricating mirror mounts
  • Calibrating the laser beam bounce system
  • Finalizing the electrical wiring
  • Documenting and reflecting



Not bad, huh? Spice Girls OUT.
